For a new leadership

I coach agile teams by practicing empathic and generative listening, so to be able to co-create while operating from a future space of possibility that wants to emerge.

This work on presence, directly inspired by Otto Scharmer Theory U, illuminates the blind spot that we all have as the source of our actions and decisions. To be effective leaders, we must first understand the field, or inner space, from which we are operating.

The ability to facilitate that shift is the essence of leadership today.
